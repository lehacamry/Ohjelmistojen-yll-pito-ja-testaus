Three Reasons to Choose LAB University of Applied Sciences

(Insights from Senior Lecturer and RDI Specialist, Malahat Ghoreishi)

1. Excellent learning opportunities and modern resources

LAB gives students the freedom to develop, improve their skills, and grow as professionals. Students are supported by teachers, tutors, and career services. The modern campuses in Lappeenranta and Lahti provide facilities for independent study and collaborative work.

2. Strong connection between studies and working life

Many teachers have experience in business and industry. Students participate in projects with companies, visit workplaces, and get to know potential employers already during their studies. This helps them build professional networks and improves their employment opportunities.
